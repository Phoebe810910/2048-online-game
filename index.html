About the Game

2048 is a classic sliding tile puzzle game. Merge tiles to reach 2048, compete with friends, and climb the global leaderboard! This version supports up to 30 concurrent players.

How to Play

  • Use Arrow Keys (↑ ↓ ← →) to slide all tiles
  • Merge identical numbers to double their value and earn points
  • A new 2/4 tile appears after each valid move
  • Game ends when no valid moves are left
  • Submit your score to the global leaderboard after game over
